Pizza Recipe

Enjoy this filling vegetarian soup with red lentils, carrots and leeks. It's low in calories and fat, and delivers three of your five-a-day


Total Calories: 219


Ingredients:

  1. 2l vegetable or ham stock
  2. 150g red lentils
  3. 6 carrots, finely chopped
  4. 2 medium leeks, sliced (about 300g)
  5. small handful of chopped parsley, to serve

Steps:

  • Step 1: Heat the stock in a large pan and add the lentils. Bring to the boil and allow the lentils to soften for a few minutes.


  • Step 2: Add the carrots and leeks, and season (don't add salt if you use ham stock as it will make it too salty). Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 45 mins-1 hr until the lentils have broken down. Scatter over the parsley and serve with buttered bread, if you like.
